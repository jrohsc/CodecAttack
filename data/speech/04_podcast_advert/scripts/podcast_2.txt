Picture a living room on a Sunday afternoon. A podcast plays through the smart speaker on the shelf. Nobody is really listening. The host talks about coffee, about weather, about whatever. Across the room, a small robot is cleaning the rug. The robot has an audio model built in. It uses speech it hears to tag rooms, to know when a person walks in, to decide whether to pause. What most people don't think about is that the robot is also running that podcast audio through its understanding pipeline, every second, just in case someone is talking to it.
